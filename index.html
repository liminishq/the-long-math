The Long Math is a growing collection of calculators and explanations designed to make personal and small-business financial trade-offs visible.

Most financial decisions are presented as stories. This site focuses on arithmetic. It examines how fees, taxes, compounding, and incentives interact over decades, using transparent assumptions and explicit calculations that can be inspected and verified.

No opinions. No hidden assumptions. Just arithmetic.
