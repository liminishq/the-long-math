Active vs passive fee comparison

This page does two things: (1) shows the break-even excess return implied by the fee difference (under this model), and (2) shows ending values under your assumed active excess return.

Short answer Break-even alpha equals (active fee − passive fee) under the net = gross − fee model. The dollar consequences depend on balance, horizon, contributions, and the alpha you assume.
